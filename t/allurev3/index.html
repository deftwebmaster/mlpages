Who We Are

Built on Relationships.
Driven by Results.

Allure Solutions, LLC is a national professional and technical recruiting firm headquartered in the Greater Dallas–Fort Worth area, specializing in Defense, Aerospace, Advanced Manufacturing, and Commercial & Industrial Construction talent acquisition.

Founded in 2005, we are a minority, woman-owned search firm with over 25 years of combined industry experience. We support organizations operating in regulated, security-sensitive, safety-driven, and mission-critical environments.

Our team is clearance-aware and experienced supporting government-contracted operations, export-controlled environments, infrastructure projects, and complex industrial builds.

"From executive leadership to highly specialized technical talent, we help companies build teams that drive operational excellence and long-term growth."
— Allure Solutions, LLC

Our Model

Contingency-Based Executive Search

Our approach is built on a contingency-based model designed to align our success directly with yours. There are no upfront fees or retained costs — we are engaged to deliver outcomes, not activity.

You only engage financially when we successfully place the right candidate. If the right match is not delivered, there is no cost. Simple, transparent, and performance-driven.
